Quick Troubleshooting Steps

1

Check Basic Connections

Ensure all cables are properly connected and secure.

  • HDMI cable is firmly connected
  • Power adapter is plugged in
  • TV is on the correct input
  • Roku device is powered on
2

Restart Your Roku Device

Power cycle your Roku device to resolve many common issues.

  • Unplug the power adapter for 10 seconds
  • Plug it back in and wait for startup
  • Check if the issue is resolved
  • Try the setup process again
3

Check Internet Connection

Verify that your internet connection is working properly.

  • Test internet on another device
  • Check WiFi signal strength
  • Restart your router if needed
  • Try connecting to a different network
4

Clear Browser Cache

If using roku.com/setup, clear your browser cache.

  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different web browser
  • Disable browser extensions
  • Try incognito/private mode
5

Contact Support

If issues persist, contact our expert support team.

  • Call our support line
  • Describe the specific issue
  • Follow expert guidance
  • Get your Roku working quickly
