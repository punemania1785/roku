Roku Setup Steps - Complete Guide

1

Connect Your Roku Device

Connect your Roku streaming device to your TV using the HDMI cable. Make sure to use the HDMI port on your TV for the best picture quality.

  • Insert the HDMI cable into your Roku device
  • Connect the other end to an available HDMI port on your TV
  • Plug in the power adapter to your Roku device
2

Power On and Select Input

Turn on your TV and select the correct HDMI input where you connected your Roku device.

  • Press the power button on your Roku device
  • Use your TV remote to select the correct HDMI input
  • Wait for the Roku logo to appear on your screen
3

Go to roku.com/setup

Visit roku.com/setup on your computer, tablet, or smartphone to begin the setup process.

  • Open your web browser
  • Navigate to roku.com/setup
  • Click "Set up your Roku device"
4

Enter Activation Code

Your TV screen will display a unique activation code. Enter this code on the roku.com/setup website.

  • Look for the 6-digit code on your TV screen
  • Enter the code exactly as shown
  • Click "Continue" to proceed
5

Connect to WiFi

Select your WiFi network and enter your password to connect your Roku device to the internet.

  • Choose your WiFi network from the list
  • Enter your WiFi password
  • Wait for the connection to be established
6

Create Roku Account

Set up your Roku account to access streaming channels and services.

  • Enter your email address
  • Create a secure password
  • Add a payment method (optional)

If the roku.com/setup page isn't loading or responding:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different web browser
  • Check your internet connection
  • Disable ad blockers temporarily

Activation code not showing

If you don't see the activation code on your TV:

  • Make sure your Roku device is properly connected
  • Check that you're on the correct HDMI input
  • Restart your Roku device
  • Try a different HDMI cable

WiFi connection problems

If you can't connect to your WiFi network:

  • Restart your router
  • Check if your network supports 2.4GHz

Roku Setup Frequently Asked Questions

How long does roku.com/setup take?

The complete Roku setup process typically takes 10-15 minutes, including roku.com/setup activation and account creation.

Do I need a credit card for Roku setup?

No, you can complete the Roku setup process without adding a payment method. You can add one later if needed.

What if roku.com/link doesn't work?

If roku.com/link isn't working, try clearing your browser cache, using a different browser, or calling our support team for assistance.

Can I set up Roku without WiFi?

You need an internet connection to complete the Roku setup process. However, you can use a mobile hotspot if WiFi isn't available.

What's the difference between roku.com/setup and roku.com/link?

roku.com/setup is for initial device setup, while roku.com/link is used for activating your device with the activation code.

How do I know if my Roku setup is complete?

Your setup is complete when you can access the Roku home screen and see available channels. You'll also receive a confirmation email.
